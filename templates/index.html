Welcome to Government Polytechnic Hamirpur Government Polytechnic Hamirpur is a reputed technical institute in Himachal Pradesh committed to providing quality diploma education in engineering and technology. The institute focuses on developing skilled professionals through practical learning, modern laboratories, and the guidance of experienced faculty members. Along with academic excellence, the college also promotes physical fitness and sportsmanship among students. Every year, the institute organizes Sports Meet during the 1st semester Which is the Fall session, where students participate in various indoor and outdoor games such as Basketball, volleyball,Kabaddi and badminton, etc. In the 2nd semester which is the summer session, the Annual Athletic Meet is conducted, encouraging students to compete in track and field events. These sporting activities continue throughout the academic every semester one after the other, helping students maintain a balance between education, health, and teamwork. Our campus environment encourages innovation, discipline, and leadership while providing opportunities for students to develop both technical and personal skills through academic activities, workshops, and extracurricular participation.
