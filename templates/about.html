About Government Polytechnic Hamirpur

Government Polytechnic Hamirpur is one of the oldest and most reputed technical institutes in Himachal Pradesh. Established in the year 1963, the institution has been continuously providing quality technical education to students from different parts of the state and neighboring regions. The college is located at Baru, Hamirpur, in a peaceful and educational environment that supports learning, innovation, discipline, and overall personality development.

The institute is affiliated with the Himachal Pradesh Takniki Shiksha Board and is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). Government Polytechnic Hamirpur offers diploma courses in various engineering and technology branches including Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology, and Internet of Things (IoT). These programs are specially designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills required in modern industries and technical fields.

The college campus includes modern laboratories, computer labs, smart classrooms, workshops, library facilities, seminar halls, playgrounds, and hostel facilities for students. The faculty members are experienced and dedicated towards helping students build strong technical foundations and professional skills. Regular practical sessions, project work, technical seminars, industrial visits, and training programs are conducted to improve students’ real-world understanding and technical confidence.

Apart from academics, Government Polytechnic Hamirpur is also highly active in sports and extracurricular activities. Every year, the institute organizes Annual Sports Meet and Athletic Meet in which students participate enthusiastically in indoor and outdoor sports competitions. Events such as football, volleyball, kabaddi, badminton, cricket, basketball, athletics races, shot put, relay races, and cultural performances are organized during these meets. These activities help students improve teamwork, leadership, discipline, physical fitness, and sportsmanship spirit.

Students of Government Polytechnic Hamirpur regularly participate in inter-polytechnic sports competitions and technical events organized across Himachal Pradesh. The institute has maintained a strong reputation for producing skilled diploma holders who work successfully in government departments, private companies, industries, and higher education institutions. Many students also continue their studies through lateral entry admissions into engineering degree colleges after completing their diploma.

Government Polytechnic Hamirpur maintains good academic and technical connections with several other government polytechnic institutions of Himachal Pradesh including Government Polytechnic Kangra, Government Polytechnic Sundernagar, Government Polytechnic Una, Government Polytechnic Rohru, Government Polytechnic Kullu, and Government Polytechnic Chamba. These institutes together contribute towards the development of technical education and skilled manpower in the state.

The institution also encourages students to participate in NSS activities, awareness campaigns, technical exhibitions, innovation projects, cultural festivals, and personality development programs. Through these activities, students gain confidence, communication skills, and leadership qualities necessary for professional life.
